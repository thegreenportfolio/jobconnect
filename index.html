JobConnect is built for practical hiring. Small businesses can find suitable people faster, and job seekers can be discovered for relevant local opportunities without the noise of traditional job boards.

For employers: hire without the noise

Add your position, start matching, and review candidates privately. Instead of posting publicly and waiting, you get direct candidate matches based on your real hiring needs — whether it’s a full-time role, specialized skills, or seasonal help.

For job seekers: stop applying everywhere

Set your desired role, location, work setup, and background once. JobConnect helps you receive relevant employer requests without endless searching.

Smart matching with real scores

JobConnect ranks matches based on role, location, work setup, and experience so you can focus on the best opportunities.

Employers send a request

When a strong match is found, employers can send a request directly to candidates. No job boards. No waiting. Just real opportunities.

Job seekers review every match

Each request includes full job details and compatibility score so you can make informed decisions.

You stay in control

Approve only the matches you want. Decline the rest. No pressure, no spam — just clean, intentional connections.

Unlock contact when both sides agree

Once both sides approve, contact details can be unlocked and the hiring process continues outside the app.

How JobConnect works

For employers: create your company profile, add a job, review matches, send requests, and unlock contact details after approval.

For job seekers: create your profile, set job preferences, review employer requests, approve the ones you like, and unlock employer contact details when ready.
